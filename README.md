## Who I Am

I'm **Inam Ali Soomro**, **Founder and CEO of [Inbyo Tech](https://inbyotech.com)** — a software development, web development, and SEO company based in Pakistan. I'm also a full-stack developer, currently completing my **BSCS at the University of Balochistan**.

Through **Inbyo Tech**, I build websites, software, mobile apps, and digital growth strategies for businesses looking to establish a strong online presence.
